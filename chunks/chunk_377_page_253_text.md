NOTICE: The driveshaft flange fits tightly on the pinion flange pilots. To make sure that the driveshaft flanges seat squarely on the pinion flange pilots, tighten the driveshaft flange bolts evenly in a cross pattern or damage to the driveshaft flange can occur. NOTE: If new bolts are not available, coat the threads of the original driveshaft flange bolts with threadlock and sealer. NOTE: Align the index marks made during removal.
