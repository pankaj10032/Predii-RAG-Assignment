Use the feeler gauge and select the thickest blade that will enter between the tool and the differential clutch pack. (cid:122) The dished or concave side of the Belleville spring must face up against the thrust face of the differential case. 9. Install the differential side gears with the differential clutch packs, selective shims and Belleville spring into the differential case.
