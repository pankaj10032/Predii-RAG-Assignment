Pinpoint Test Q: DTC U0155 Refer to Wiring Diagrams Cell 42 , Vehicle Dynamic Systems for schematic and connector information. Normal Operation (cid:122) DTC U0155 (Lost Communication With Instrument Panel Cluster (IC) Control Module) —this DTC sets in continuous memory and on-demand if the traction control switch information, or other sensor messages from the IPC are not received for 5 seconds or longer. This pinpoint test is intended to diagnose the following: (cid:122) Wiring, terminals or connectors (cid:122) IPC (cid:122) ABS module
