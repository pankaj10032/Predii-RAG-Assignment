2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 1sur 1 SECTION 206-03: Front Disc Brake 2014 F-150 Workshop Manual DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION Procedure revision date: 10/25/2013 Front Disc Brake The front brake disc system consists of the following components: (cid:122) Brake pads (cid:122) Brake caliper anchor plate (cid:122) Brake caliper (cid:122) Brake disc (cid:122) Brake disc shield (cid:122) Brake flexible hose When mechanical force is applied by the driver to the brake pedal, the force is converted into hydraulic pressure by the master cylinder. The hydraulic force is directed to the disc brake calipers and transferred to the brake pads. The brake pads are then forced against the brake friction surfaces by the brake caliper pistons.
