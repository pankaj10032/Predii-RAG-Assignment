2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 1sur 2 SECTION 206-05: Parking Brake and Actuation 2014 F-150 Workshop Manual GENERAL PROCEDURES Procedure revision date: 10/25/2013 Parking Brake Cable Tension Release 1. Release the parking brake. 2. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100- 02 . 3. With an assistant, release the parking brake cable tension by pulling down on the intermediate cable at the cable-to-cable union until the parking brake control sector rotates to its stop and a 4 mm (0.15 in) x 150 mm (5.9 in) retainer pin can be inserted. 4. Disconnect the cable at the cable-to-cable union. 5. To reload the tension on the parking brake cable, follow the release procedure in reverse.
