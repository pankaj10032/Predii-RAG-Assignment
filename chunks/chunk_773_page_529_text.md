2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 17sur 27 18. Position the pinion flange. 19. Using the Drive Pinion Flange Installer, install the pinion flange. 20. Apply a small amount of axle lubricant to the washer side of the new pinion nut and install the new pinion nut. 21. NOTICE: Do not loosen the pinion nut to reduce drive pinion bearing preload under any circumstance. If it is necessary to reduce drive pinion bearing preload, install a new drive pinion collapsible spacer and pinion nut or damage to the component may occur. Install the Drive Pinion Flange Holding Fixture and tighten the pinion nut. Refer to the torque specifications in the Specifications portion of this section. (cid:122) Rotate the drive pinion gear occasionally to make sure the drive pinion bearings are seating correctly.
