2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 1sur 2 SECTION 205-01: Driveshaft 2014 F-150 Workshop Manual DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION Procedure revision date: 10/25/2013 Universal Joints U-joints have the following features: (cid:122) Lubed-for-life design and require no lubrication (cid:122) Equipped with nylon thrust washers (located at each base of the bearing cup) which control end play, position the needle bearing and improve grease movement (cid:122) Held in place by snap rings The U-joints allow the speeding up and slowing down of the driveshafts as the angularity of the driveshaft changes.
