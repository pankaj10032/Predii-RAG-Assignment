# General
**Page:** 158 | **Type:** text | **Parent:** General

2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 6sur 19 (cid:122) If a phone charger is causing an RFI issue, the customer should consult their cell phone provider to acquire a different phone charger. (cid:122) If a device such as a dealer-installed alarm is causing an RFI issue, move the device to another location on the vehicle. In the case of a portable device, move the power cord to another power point location. In summary, if the RFI source is present and cannot be moved or replaced, the intermittent issue remains. The TPMS system must accept RFI and the unwanted system operation it can cause.
