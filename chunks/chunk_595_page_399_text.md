2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 10sur 12 8. NOTE: Drive pinion bearing adjustment shims must be flat and clean. NOTE: A slight drag should be felt for correct drive pinion bearing adjustment shim selection. Do not attempt to force the drive pinion bearing adjustment shim between the gauge block and the gauge tube. This will minimize selection of a drive pinion bearing adjustment shim thicker than required, which results in a deep tooth contact in the final assembly of integral axle assemblies. Use a drive pinion bearing adjustment shim as a gauge for drive pinion bearing adjustment shim selection. (cid:122) After the correct drive pinion bearing adjustment shim thickness has been determined, remove the Adapters. 9. Using the Bearing/Oil Seal Plate and Drive Pinion Bearing Cone Installer, press the drive pinion bearing and selected drive pinion bearing adjustment shim until they are firmly seated on the pinion gear. 10.
