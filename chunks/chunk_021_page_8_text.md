Ball Joint Inspection 1. Prior to inspecting the ball joints for wear, inspect the wheel bearings. Refer to Section 100-04 . 2. NOTE: In order to obtain accurate measurements, the suspension must be in full rebound with the weight of the vehicle supported by the frame. Raise and support the vehicle by the frame to allow the wheels to hang in the rebound position. 3. Inspect the ball joint and ball joint boot for damage. (cid:122) If the ball joint or ball joint boot is damaged, install a new ball joint as necessary.
