Pinpoint Test H: DTC B1251:00 Normal Operation (cid:122) DTC B1251:00 (Tire Pressure Sensor Low Battery: No Sub Type Information) —This DTC sets in continuous memory in the Body Control Module (BCM) when there is a fault in the Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS), such as a damaged or missing TPMS sensor(s), or when attempting to train a TPMS sensor(s) with a low battery. The TPMS warning indicator flashes for 70 seconds then illuminates continuously when the ignition switch is turned to the ON position. The message center also displays TIRE PRESSURE SENSOR FAULT.
