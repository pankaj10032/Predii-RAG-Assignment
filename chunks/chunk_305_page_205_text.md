# 2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 2sur 18

**Page:** 205 | **Type:** text

2014 F-150 Workshop Manual Page 2sur 18 joints or CV joints at the ends of the shafts allow the shafts to rotate smoothly in an allowable angle plane. The rotational torque is introduced into the axle drive pinion which drives the differential ring gear. The ring gear is bolted to the differential case flange on the differential. The differential divides the torque between the right and left halfshaft while permitting the halfshafts to turn at different speeds when required, such as when cornering.
