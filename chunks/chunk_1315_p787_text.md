Pinpoint Test J: The Hill Descent Control System Is Inoperative Refer to Wiring Diagrams Cell 42 , Vehicle Dynamic Systems for schematic and connector information. Normal Operation Hill Descent Control™ can be activated by momentarily pressing the Hill Descent Control™ switch at speeds from 3 km/h (2 mph) and 16 km/h (10 mph), but the system stays in control up to 32 km/h (20 mph). At speeds between 32 km/h (20 mph) and 64 km/h (40 mph), Hill Descent Control™ does not set or maintain speeds.
