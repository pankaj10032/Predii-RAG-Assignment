Symptom Chart NOTE: For vehicles with different front and rear tire pressures, the tire pressures must be adjusted and the tire pressure sensors must be trained following a tire rotation. Failure to train the sensors results in a false low tire pressure event, causing the Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) indicator to illuminate.
